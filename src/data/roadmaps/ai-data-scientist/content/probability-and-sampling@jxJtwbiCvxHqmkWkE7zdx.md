# Probability and Sampling

Probability is the measure of how likely an event is to occur, expressed as a number between 0 and 1. Sampling involves selecting a subset of individuals from within a statistical population to estimate characteristics of the whole population. These techniques are fundamental for understanding data distributions, making predictions, and drawing inferences from data. They provide the basis for hypothesis testing, confidence intervals, and many machine learning algorithms.
