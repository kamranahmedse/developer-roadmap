# Career Progression

Design Engineering offers a clear career path from junior to staff levels, with compensation competitive with senior frontend engineering roles.

**Junior Design Engineer** (0-2 years):
- Strong portfolio demonstrating design sense and code quality
- Execute designs with guidance, learn systems
- Expected to grow rapidly with mentorship
- Salary: $67K-$150K depending on location/company

**Mid Design Engineer** (3-5 years):
- Own features end-to-end
- Make independent technical decisions
- Contribute to design system architecture
- Mentor juniors, collaborate directly with designers
- Salary: $145K-$200K

**Senior Design Engineer** (5+ years):
- Technical leadership and vision
- Shape design system strategy
- Partner with design leadership
- Mentor team members
- Salary: $180K-$250K+

**Staff Design Engineer** (7+ years):
- Set technical direction across teams
- Shape company design engineering culture
- Industry influence through talks, writing, open source
- Salary: $200K-$300K+
