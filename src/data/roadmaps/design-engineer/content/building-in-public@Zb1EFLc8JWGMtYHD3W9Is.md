# Building in Public

Building in public—sharing your learning journey, projects, and insights openly—accelerates career growth and creates opportunities. Many Design Engineers get hired through their public work.

**What to Share**:
- Work in progress and iterations
- Things you learned (TIL posts)
- Component recreations and experiments
- Design system contributions
- Open source work
- Thoughtful commentary on design/engineering

**Platforms**:
- **Twitter/X**: Quick updates, engagement with community
- **Blog**: Long-form thinking, tutorials, case studies
- **GitHub**: Code contributions, project showcases
- **YouTube**: Tutorials, process videos
- **CodePen/CodeSandbox**: Interactive demos

**Benefits**:
- Builds reputation and recognition
- Creates serendipitous opportunities
- Forces you to understand things deeply
- Connects you with like-minded people
- Documents your growth over time

**Getting Started**:
- Share something small today
- Don't wait until it's "perfect"
- Engage with others' work
- Be consistent over time
