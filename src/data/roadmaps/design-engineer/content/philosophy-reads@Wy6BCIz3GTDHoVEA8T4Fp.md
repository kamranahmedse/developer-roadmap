# Philosophy Reads

These foundational texts shape how you think about design and development. They're not tutorialsâ€”they're philosophical frameworks that inform every decision you make as a Design Engineer. Read them throughout your learning journey, not just once.

**Resilient Web Design** by Jeremy Keith: Explains why the web works the way it does. Understanding the web's resilience and progressive nature shapes your approach to HTML, CSS, and accessibility.

**Refactoring UI** by Adam Wathan & Steve Schoger: The most practical design resource for developers. Every page is actionable tactics, not abstract theory. "Use color and weight to create hierarchy instead of size."

**A Dao of Web Design** by John Allsopp: A 2000 essay that predicted responsive design. "We should embrace the fact that the web doesn't have the same constraints as print."

**Just JavaScript** by Dan Abramov & Maggie Appleton: Rebuilds how you think about JavaScript from first principles. Visual mental models that make complex concepts intuitive.
