# What is Design Engineering?

Design Engineering is a hybrid discipline that bridges the gap between design and frontend development. Design Engineers are not "designers who code" or "developers with good taste"â€”they are professionals who are native to both worlds, with deep expertise in crafting polished, production-ready user interfaces.

Design Engineers obsess over the details that make interfaces feel alive: micro-interactions, transitions, loading states, and the subtle polish that distinguishes exceptional products. They think in systems, building reusable components and design tokens that scale across products.

Key characteristics of Design Engineers:

- **Systems Thinkers**: Build reusable, accessible component systems
- **Detail Obsessed**: Care about hover states, transitions, and micro-interactions
- **Technically Skilled**: Write production-quality React, TypeScript, and CSS
- **Design Literate**: Understand visual design principles and can work directly with Figma
- **User Advocates**: Prioritize accessibility and user experience
