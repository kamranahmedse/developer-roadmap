# Spiral Learning Approach

This roadmap uses spiral learningâ€”a methodology where you revisit core concepts multiple times at increasing depth. Rather than mastering one topic before moving to the next, you build a mental map of everything first, then deepen your understanding with each pass.

**Phase 1 - Foundation (4 weeks)**: Touch all major concepts at surface level. Build your mental map of how everything connects. You won't master anything yet, and that's okay.

**Phase 2 - Building (6 weeks)**: Return to the same topics with more depth. Build real projects that combine multiple concepts. Start to understand "why" not just "how."

**Phase 3 - Professional (8 weeks)**: Advanced patterns and production-quality work. Design systems, complex animations, accessibility mastery. Ready for professional work.

**Phase 4 - Mastery (Ongoing)**: Specialization, portfolio development, and career growth. Continuous learning and refinement.

**Why Spiral Works**: Traditional linear learning leads to forgetting earlier topics while grinding on later ones. Spiral learning reinforces connections and builds deeper understanding through repetition with variation.
