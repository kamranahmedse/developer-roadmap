# Distribution Shape 

In the realm of Data Analysis, the distribution shape is considered as an essential component under descriptive analysis. A data analyst uses the shape of the distribution to understand the spread and trend of the data set. It aids in identifying the skewness (asymmetry) and kurtosis (the 'tailedness') of the data and helps to reveal meaningful patterns that standard statistical measures like mean or median might not capture.
